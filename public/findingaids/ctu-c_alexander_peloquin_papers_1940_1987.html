Biographical Information:

C. Alexander Peloquin was born June 16, 1918, and died February 27, 1997. He is known for composing the first Roman Catholic Mass sung in English, and was a significant composer of liturgical music. During his liturgical music career, he directed and participated in performances of his compositions, and was also a pianist and an organist.
