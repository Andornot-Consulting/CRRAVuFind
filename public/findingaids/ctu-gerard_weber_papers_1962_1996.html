Biographical Information:Rev. Gerard P. Weber served in both the Archdioceses of Chicago and Los Angeles. As a writer of catechetical materials, his most well known work, Life in Christ, was co-authored with Rev. James Killgallon. Scope and Contents

Some of Gerard Weber's manuscripts for catechetical materials are in the collection, as well as his correspondence, including that between Weber and Cardinal John Cody. Other documents include ACTA Foundation agendas, minutes, budgets, by-laws, contracts, and documents related to the sale of ACTA.
