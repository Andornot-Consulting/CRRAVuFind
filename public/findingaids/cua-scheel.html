Biographical Note

Nivard Scheel, C.F.X. was born November 8, 1925 in Baltimore, Maryland. He entered the Xavarian Brothers on June 30, 1943, in which afterwards he attained his A.B., 1949, M.S, 1951, and PhD 1961 in Physics at the Catholic University of America (CUA). He was president of Xavarian College in Silver Spring, Maryland, 1960-1966. He then went on to be principal at Nazareth High School in Brooklyn 1966-1968 before being appointed Assistant to the Acting Rector by Fr. John P. Whalen in 1967 at CUA. From 1968 to1969 he was Acting Rector of CUA before being appointed by President Clarence Walton as Vice President for Student Affairs in 1970.

Scope and Contents

The Nivard Scheel Collection consists of personal papers from his time as a CUA student. Included are many class notes and notebooks with handwritten notes, computer printouts, experiment reports, and blue book tests (twenty-five in total).
