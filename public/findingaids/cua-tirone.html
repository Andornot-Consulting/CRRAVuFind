Biographical Note

Rosemary (Macri) Tirone attended The Catholic University of America starting in 1953, and graduated with a BSN in 1957. While at CUA, she was involved with the Alpha Alpha Chapter of the Theta Phi Alpha Fraternity, and the Laboure Hall Association, a CUA Nursing Society.

She currently lives in New York.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains photographs, a scrapbook, CUA publications, and nursing caps from 1953 to 1957. The scrapbook contains primarily photographs, and notations about the photographs.

This collection is divided into three series: 1) Photographs, 2) Publications, and 3) 3-D Objects.
