Descriptive Summary

Creator: Lawrence Frederick Wright
Title: The Lawrence F. Wright Photograph Collection
Dates: ca. 1917-1921
Abstract: Lawrence Frederick Wright, a member of The Catholic University of America class of 1921, compiled the 78 images in this collection during his years at CUA from 1917 to 1921. The images are all black and white with Mr. Wright the photographer for many of them. This collection contains images of the CUA buildings and grounds, students and professors on campus, and student events both on and off campus. Many of the images appear to have been used in the 1917-1921 editions of the Cardinal, the CUA yearbook.
Extent: 0.9 linear feet; 1 box
Language: English
Identification: ACUA 215

Administrative Information

Acquisition Information:

Donated by the family of Lawrence F. Wright in 2005. The images were originally house in an album that had deteriorated over time. The original order of images, their placement on each page, and any legiable captions were recorded. Images are presented in the order that they originaly appeared in the album when it arrived at ACUA.

Biographical Note

Lawrence Frederick Wright, was a native of Brooklyn, New York. He served briefly as a lieutenant in the U.S. Army and was also a student at The Catholic University of America (CUA) in Washington, DC. He studied at CUA from 1917 to 1921, earning a Bachelor degree in Science.

Scope and Contents

The 78 images are all black and white, and Mr. Wright was the photographer for many of the images. This collection contains images of the CUA buildings and grounds, students and professors on campus, and student events both on and off CUA grounds. Many of the images in this collection appear to have been used in the 1917-1921 editions of the Cardinal, the CUA yearbook.
