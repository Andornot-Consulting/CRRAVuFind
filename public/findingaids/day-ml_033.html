Title: Josef Levit collection on Marian art
Dates: circa 1934-2000
Abstract: This collection includes postcards, photographs, holy cards, and clippings depicting Marian shrines, paintings, statues, and other Catholic artwork. The collection primarily covers Marian art in western Europe during the second half of the 20th century.

Biography of Josef Levit, S.M.

Josef Levit, S.M. is a member of the Society of Mary based in Austria. He is the author of Blessed Jakob Gapp: Marianist, published by the North American Center for Marianist Studies in 1998.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains photographs, greeting cards, postcards, holy cards, and clippings depicting Marian shrines, paintings, and statues. The materials are accompanied by handwritten descriptions in German. The majority of the collection includes photographs taken during travel or pilgrimages to Marian shrines around Europe.
