Biography of Father Bertrand Clemens, S.M.

Bertrand E. Clemens was born January 13, 1917. He graduated from the University of Dayton in 1938. In 1956 he was appointed director of the Institute of Marianist Studies in Glencoe, Missouri. He later relocated to the West Coast where he served at Junipero Serra High School in Gardena, California. In 1964 he was appointed Provincial of the Pacific Province of the Marianists. In 1975 he returned to Junipero Serra High School to serve as Principal. He also served as Associate Pastor at Maria Regina Catholic Church in Gardena. In 1985 Fr. Clemens was appointed Pastor of Queen of Apostles Parish in San Jose, California. He passed away February 1, 1989 in San Jose, California.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains a series of lectures on Mariology given by Fr. Bertrand E. Clemens, S.M. in 1982. The lectures, on 13 audio cassettes, cover topics including Mary's Virginity, Cana, Calvary, Discipleship, Mary's Humanness, and the Magnificat, among others.
