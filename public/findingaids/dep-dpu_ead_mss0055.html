Biographical Note:

Oscar Romero became archbishop of San Salvador, El Savador on February 22, 1977. He was murdured on March 24, 1980. During Romero's tenure as archbishop, He became an outspoken voice of the poor Catholic people of El Salvador. He was both a hero to Church members and an enemy of the military rulers of El Salvador.
