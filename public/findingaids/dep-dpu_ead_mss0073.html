Biographical Note

Murray Polner is one of the authors of Disarmed and Dangerous: The Radical Life and Times of Daniel and Philip Berrigan. Other works by Polner include Branch Rickey: A Biography, No Victory Parades: The Return of the Vietnam Veteran, and We Who Said No to War.

Description of the Collection

The Murray Polner papers reflect the research of Polner as he wrote Disarmed and Dangerous: The Radical Life and Times of Daniel and Philip Berrigan. Research materials for the book included in this collection were primarily gathered by Polner in 1993-1996 from publications, archives, the Berrigan family, those who knew them, and Polner's collection of materials on the peace and anti-nuclear movements. Reactions to the release of Disarmed and Dangerous are also included.

Arrangement of the Collection

The Murray Polner papers are arranged into six series: 1. Berrigan Biographical Files; 2. Peace Actions; 3. People Files; 4. Subject Files; 5. Notes and Research; 6. June 2013 Addition. Most series are further divided into subseries; see series level descriptions for further details. Arrangement of the materials is based on Polner's original organization, which includes folder labels present at the time materials were donated, as well as Polner's annotations on materials that were not in labeled folders. When neither folder labels nor item annotations were available, items were examined by the processor to determine their placement. Within the series, materials are arranged to reflect where Polner collected them, including published materials such as clippings and book excerpts, archival sources such as Cornell University and government agencies, and individuals who provided writings and recollections such as Daniel Berrigan or Liz McAlister. The June 2013 Addition series includes all of those materials from accession 2013.21.

Series 001: Berrigan Biographical Files 1959-2011

The Berrigan Biographical series includes news clippings, archival research, book excerpts, and notes supporting Polner's biographical description of the Berrigans. These papers relate to the Berrigans' general backgrounds and lives and do not include material on the Berrigans' peace actions.

The Berrigan Biographical series is divided into three subseries: 1. Berrigan Family; 2. Daniel Berrigan; 3. Philip Berrigan. The Berrigan Family subseries contains material on both Daniel and Philip Berrigan or other Berrigan Family members. The Daniel Berrigan subseries includes research on Daniel Berrigan, as well as his poetry and prose writings and commentary on them. The Philip Berrigan subseries includes writings from and about Philip Berrigan and his time in the Federal Correctional Institution in Danbury, Connecticut.

Series 002.009: Plowshares 1973-1999

The Plowshares subseries includes materials on the Plowshares peace/anti-nuclear movement founded by Daniel and Philip Berrigan. The Plowshares 8 peace action involving Daniel and Philip Berrigan and six others at a General Electric facility in King of Prussia, Pennsylvania on September 9, 1980 is documented in this subseries. Folders in this subseries on this and other actions performed as part of the Plowshares movement are in chronological order by the date of each action. General Plowshares movement information, as well as a book proposal for an unpublished work by Polner on the Plowshares movement, Breaking the Silence, are also included. Plowshares-Disarmament Actions, a newsletter in subseries 2.10, may be particularly useful for understanding the Plowshares movement. It contains a chronological overview of plowshares actions listing dates, names, and locations of actions and participants.

Folders in this subseries are arranged chronologically by the date of the plowshares action. Folders with materials related to the Plowshares movement or multiple actions are filed at the end.
