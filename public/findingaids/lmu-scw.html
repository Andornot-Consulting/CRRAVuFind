Collection Description

This collection consists of pamphlets relating to anti-Communism and the Spanish Civil War of 1936-39. Published between 1913 and 1961, the pamphlets, in general, take pro-Franco, pro-Catholic Church and strongly anti-Communist positions. Some post-Civil War pamphlets (eg, Report on Spain, published in 1946) represent the government of Franco's apologia for their policies in the war. All are in English, except one, which is in Spanish.
