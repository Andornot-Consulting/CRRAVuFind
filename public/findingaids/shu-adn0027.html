Abstract: The Catholic Women's College Club was a New Jersey organization in operation from at least 1934-1985. This collection spans the years of 1934 through 1985 and documents the development and subsequent operations of the Catholic Women's College Club, a New Jersey organization. The collection includes records of meetings and activities as well as clippings and memorabilia.

Biographical/Historical note

The Catholic Women's College Club was a New Jersey organization in operation from at least 1934-1985. The organization hosted many events and activities for its members. It is unclear when exactly the organization became defunct.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Contents note

This collection spans the years of 1934 through 1985 and documents the development and subsequent operations of the Catholic Women's College Club. Included are meeting minutes, newspaper clippings mentioning the group, and programs, invitations and other mementos from their activities. Minutes from the first meeting document the discussion of the creation of a Constitution and this document is also among the materials in the Goverment files. The newspaper clippings, some of which contain photographs, mention members of the group and meeting dates and events.

Arrangement note

Much of the material is then organized chronologically as demonstrated in the Meeting Minutes series, or categorically when appropriate, such as in the Goverment and Finance series. The sixth series, Programs, Bulletins, and Memorabilia contains the sub-series of Monthly Bulletins, By Year, and June Supper Meetings.

The collection is arranged as follows:

  • Series I. Government and finance
  • Series II. Meeting minutes
  • Series III. Records of Corresponding Secretary
  • Series IV. Clippings
  • Series V. Correspondence
  • Series VI. Programs, bulletins, memorabilia
  • Series VII. Artifacts
