Background

Professor of Government at the University of Notre Dame; author of The rise of African nationalism in South Africa: the African National Congress, 1912-1952; The Christian Institute and the resurgence of African Nationalism; Church versus state in South Africa: the case of the Christian Institute; and Prophetic Christianity and the liberation movement in South Africa .

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Collected material in support of research into Church - State conflicts in twentieth-century South Africa. Also papers having to do with Notre Dame's divestment in South Africa.
