Background

Catholic weekly magazine founded by Edward F. Sorin in 1865. Daniel E. Hudson served as editor from 1875-1928.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Chiefly manuscripts submitted for publication, 1890s - 1920s, with work by John Ayscough, A.M. Berlinquet, Nora Byeman, L. Joan Chubb, the Countess de Courson, Charles A. Dobson, Joseph Dutton, Maurice Francis Egan, M. de la Fontaine, Rose Hawthorne Lathrop, Marie Marchal, Edward Wilbur Mason, Marian Nesbitt, M. Barry O'Delany, Christian Reid, Nigel Robinson, Magdalen Rock, Anna T. Sadlier, Charles Warren Stoddard, and others; Also editorial correspondence, 1895-1929; a ledger listing Ave Maria subscribers, 1894-1896; and manuscripts of obituaries, 1891.

Also a bound typewritten index to Ave Maria magazine, 1865-1924, compiled by Mildred Florence Baumgartner in 1945; and bound volumes of Ave Maria magazine, 1865-1970, and A.D. Correspondence, 1970-1979.

PAVE Ave Maria Magazine: Printed Material

Bound volumes of Ave Maria magazine, 1865-1970. In 1945 Mildred Florence Baumgartner compiled and produced on her typewriter an index to the magazine, 1865-1924, the bound volumes of which we keep after the bound volumes of the magazine itself.

Also bound volumes of a related magazine, A.D. Correspondence, published by the congregation of Holy Cross from 1970 to 1979.
