Scope and Content

General files of publisher John L. Reedy, CSC, 1953-1983, editorial files 1956-1983, and financial records 1940-1983; including correspondence, biographical profiles of authors, permissions, diaries, agenda, logs, lists, reprints, and records of advertising, publicity, and circulation; with material concerning conferences and meetings, bishops' synods, syndicated columns, Today magazine, A.D. magazine, the Ave Maria Board of Governors, the relationship between Ave Maria and the University of Notre Dame, the Catholic Press Association, and King's College of Wilkes Barre, Pennsylvania.
