Background

Catholic priest, member of the Congregation of Holy Cross (CSC); biology professor at the University of Notre Dame (1948, 1950-1964), and visiting research professor at the Argonne National Laboratory (1949), an Atomic Energy Commission (AEC) facility near Chicago.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence (1938-1964) with AEC officials, colleagues, and students; grant applications, contracts, reports and other material relating to his experimentation in radiation biology and neuro-physiology for the AEC (1951-1964); personal documents; a scrapbook; and photographs.
