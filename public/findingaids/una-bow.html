Background

Jesuit priest; author of U.S. Catholic Ecumenism -- Ten Years Later ; co-author, with Lynne Shivers, of More Than The Troubles : a Common Sense View of the Northern Ireland Conflict .

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence, memoranda, newsletters, notes, and material distributed at meetings; with files on Northern Ireland, Ecumenism, Jesuits, Pax Christi, Justice and Peace, Common Liturgical Texts for Christians, and interaction with bishops and other priests; manuscripts and research files for Bowman's book More Than the Troubles: A Common Sense View of the Northern Ireland Conflict (1984); Irish, Irish-American, and other newspapers, magazines, catalogs, brochures, reports, maps, and journals having to do with Northern Ireland and the troubles there, including the Church of Ireland Gazette , the Irish Echo , and the Irish Voice ; photographs of Ireland; and related audio and video tapes; files on subjects of interest to Bowman, including the Second Vatican Council, theology, abortion, and human sexuality; and files of the National Council of Churches of Christ generated by Bowman during his affiliation with it.
