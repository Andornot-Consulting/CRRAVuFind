Background

In 1965 Sally Cunneen conducted a survey on women's issues with a population consisting mainly of Cross Currents magazine readers. She published a book based on the survey, Sex: Female; Religion: Catholic (New York, Holt, Rinehart and Winston, 1968).

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Working files from Sally Cunneen's 1965 survey of readers of Cross Currents magazine; consisting of the coding book, notebooks, completed survey questionnaires, and computer printouts.
