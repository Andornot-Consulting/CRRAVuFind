MCEM Notre Dame: Cemeteries: Microfilm 1800s-1989

Microfilmed by the Genealogical Society of Utah in March and July 1989 as reels #1617244 and #1637530 - #1637533 (which are now M522, reels 5 and 1 - 4, respectively).

MCEM reels #1-4 Cedar Grove Cemetery Records:

MCEM reel #1 Funerals Book [117+ pages] 7/1932 - 4/1938

Shows date of funeral, name, lot location, and charges. Arranged by date of funeral.

MCEM reel #1 Record of Interments [101+ pages] 4/1938 - 10/1964

Shows name, residence, age at death, date of death, date of burial, undertaker, plot location, fees, and nearest relative or friend. Arranged by date of burial.

MCEM reel #1 Account Book [117+ pages] 3/1894 - 6/1899

MCEM reel #1 Account Book [139+ pages] 1/1898 - 12/1901

MCEM reel #1 Account Book [85+ pages] 1/1898 - 12/1903

This one volume consists entirely of [outstanding?] accounts transferred from two "old books".

MCEM reel #1 Account Book [550+ pages] 11/1899 - 10/1914

MCEM reel #1 Account Book [61+ pages] 1/1902 - 12/1902

MCEM reel #1 Account Book [272+ pages] 7/1902 - 12/1906

Each of these volume is arranged roughly by date and shows name, address, date, charges (for burials, lots, vaults, caskets, hearse rental, etc.), and payments. Some volumes also have accounts with funeral directors, suppliers, etc. Each volume contains an index at the front of the volume.

MCEM reel #1 Index to Lots [? pages] probably late 19th/ early 20th century

MCEM reel #1 Index to Lots [65 pages] probably late 19th/ early 20th century

MCEM reel #1 Index to Lots [83 pages] probably late 19th/ early 20th century

Each volume shows name and lot number and includes maps of the cemetery and other records. Arranged by initial letter of surname. The contents of the three volumes overlap considerably.

MCEM reel #2 Account Book [263 pages] (7/98-) 9/1914 - 9/1930

Shows name, date, address, charges, and payments. Earlier years arranged by date, later years by year, then funeral director, then date. Back of book has accounts with funeral directors. Contains an index at the front of the volume.

MCEM reel #2 Index to Lot Owners / Burials [?]

Two parts. The first shows name, sometimes address, and lot location and is arranged by initial letter of surname; the second shows name and lot location and is arranged by lot number.

MCEM reel #2 List of Lots

Shows family name and lot number. Arranged by section and lot number. This list probbaly dates after 1970.

MCEM reel #2 Lot Record Sheets, Sections A-B probably late 19th/ early 20th century

MCEM reel #3 Lot Record Sheets, Sections C-E probably late 19th/ early 20th century

MCEM reel #4 Lot Record Sheets, Sections F-H

Shows name of lot owner; name, date, age, and grave number of each burial in the lot; description of each monumnet; and a sketch of the locations of the graves within the lot. Arranged by section and lot number. See finding aid for a printout of a sample page.

MCEM reel #5 Lists of Burials at and around Notre Dame:

Items 1,2,and 3 on M522 #5 relate to a church in Elkhart, for which see MPIC

MCEM reel #5 Cedar Grove Cemetery - Report (Index) of Transactions 1800s-1989

A computer printout showing name, age, section and lot number, date of transaction, type of transaction (purchase of lot, interment, vault, marker foundation, etc.). Arranged alphabetically by name. See finding aid for an explanation of codes and a sample page.

MCEM reel #5 Our Lady of Peace Cemetery, St. Mary's - Record Book 1850-1989

Shows name and year of death for each sister (and the few non-religious) buried in the cemetery. Arranged by section, row and lot number.

MCEM reel #5 List of CSC Sisters Interred Away from Saint Mary's 1855-1988

Shows name, year of death, and location of grave. Arranged by year of death.

MCEM reel #5 Holy Cross Community Cemetery - List of Interments 1800s-1988

Shows plot number, name, birth date, and death date. Arranged alphabetically by name through 1981 and chronologically by date of death for 1982-88.

MCEM reel #5 St. Joseph Cemetery - List of Brothers Buried There 1966-1988

Shows name, birth date, and death date. Arranged alphabetically by name. This Cemetery is at Holy Cross Junior College, Notre Dame, IN.

MCEM reel #5 Special Burials in Sacred Heart Bascilica and Log Chapel 1989

A list of and historical notes about the persons buried within these two buildings.
