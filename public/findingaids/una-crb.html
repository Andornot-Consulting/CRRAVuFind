Scope and Content

Photostats and transcriptions of medieval manuscripts: the Glossa Ordinaria on Proverbs and Ecclesiastes, Hugh of St. Cher on Proverbs, Stephen Langton on Proverbs, Andrew of St. Victor on Proverbs, Guerric of St. Quintin on Proverbs, and William of Auvergne on Proverbs and Ecclesiastes.
