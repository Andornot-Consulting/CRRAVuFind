Background

Founded in 1974 as an organization of married Roman Catholic priests, CORPUS now defines itself as the national association for an inclusive priesthood and includes lay people and women among its members. CORPUS "promotes an expanded and renewed priesthood of married and single men and women in the Catholic Church" (CORPUS Brochure).

Scope and Content

Correspondence, Board meeting files, research files, conference files, subject files, Vatican II Council Daybooks, surveys, reports, press releases, Corpus directories, circulars, bulletins, newsletters, Priestly Ministry USA, and other printed material.
