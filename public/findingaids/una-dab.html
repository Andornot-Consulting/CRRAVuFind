Background

Established in 1847, the Diocese of Albany is a suffragan see of the Archdiocese of New York. John McCloskey served as the first bishop (1847-1864) and John J. Conroy as the second (1865-1877).

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Miscellaneous papers having to do with the Diocese of Albany or with activities within it; with material relating to bishops John McCloskey and John J. Conroy.
