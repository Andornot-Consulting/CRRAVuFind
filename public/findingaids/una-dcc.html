Background

The Vicariate Apostolic of Brownsville, established in 1874 with Bishops Dominic Manucy (1874-1884) and Peter Verdaguer (1890-1911) as its Vicars Apostolic, became the Diocese of Corpus Christi in 1912. Paul J. Nussbaum, C.P., served as its first bishop (1912-1921) and Emmanuel B. Ledvina as its second (1921-1949).

Scope and Content

"History of the Diocese of Corpus Christi, Texas" by Sister Mary Xavier Holworthy (a Master's Thesis at St. Mary's Univesrity of San Antonio, Texas, August 1939); and a map of the Diocese of Corpus Christi associated with the thesis.
