Background

Established by Pope Pius IX as the Diocese of Galveston in 1847, it had John M. Odin as its first bishop, Claude M. Dubuis (in 1861) as its second, Nicholas A. Gallagher (in 1892) as its third, and Christopher E. Byrne (in 1918) as its fourth.

Scope and Content

Collected papers having to do with activities in the territory of the Diocese of Galveston-Houston.
