Background

Professor of theology at the University of San Francisco, 1970-1971; professor of history at Notre Dame, 1971-2004 (Professor Emeritus 2002-); founder and director of the Cushwa Center for the Study of American Catholicism, 1975-1993.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence, 1966-1993, documenting Dolan's activity as a student and professor, and subject files from his research and professional activities, 1962-1993.
