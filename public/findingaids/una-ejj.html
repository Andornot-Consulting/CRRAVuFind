Scope and Content

Papers collected by Eric J. Jumper concerning scientific study of the Shroud of Turin and use of technology to produce a three-dimensional model based on data latent in the shroud. In addition to files supporting and documenting the Shroud of Turin Research Project, this collection contains books, periodicals, and clippings having to do with the Shroud of Turin, publicity for the research project, background publications, multi-media programs, video and audio tapes, slides, x-rays, prints, negatives, and other images of the Shroud of Turin. With the papers came two large three-dimensional models produced by the research project.
