Background

The Erasmus Institute was an international center at Notre Dame that helped scholars who were cultivating Catholic intellectual and cultural traditions, as well as those who would become such scholars. It offered fellowships, seminars, lectures, and conferences. The Institute invited participants without regard to their religious belief.
