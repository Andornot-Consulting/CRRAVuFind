Background

Merchant, regional manager (1932-1933) and vice president (1933-1939) for Montgomery Ward, member Council of National Defense Advisory Commission, (1940-1941), chief procurement officer of the U.S. Navy (1941-1943), executive with RCA serving as president (1949-1957), and delegate of the Holy See (Vatican) to the International Atomic Energy Agency (1957-1969).

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence, 1923-1960s; speeches by Folsom, 1940s; diaries and daily logs, 1940s-1960s; photographs, scrapbooks, and clippings involving Folsom, 1943-1963; and printed material including reports of the International Atomic Energy Agency, 1957-1963.
