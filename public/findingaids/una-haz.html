Background

Poet, essayist, novelist, professor at Duquesne University, and director of the International Poetry Forum in Pittsburgh.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence with Archibald MacLeish, John Ciardi, Princess Grace of Monaco, and others who participated in the programs of the International Poetry Forum; correspondence with Jacques Maritain; journals containing manuscript drafts of poems and fiction by Hazo; manuscripts and galley proofs of his books; periodicals and books containing his work; books published by the International Poetry Forum; and 18 audio tapes, most of them from Hazo's radio program, featuring interviews with prominent writers.
