Background

Humorist published by Sheed & Ward; wife of Notre Dame professor Louis Hasley.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

One note from Thomas Merton (Fr. Louis) to Hasley, November 1949; three letters from Caryll Houselander to Hasley, 1948-1949; Black and white photos of Hasley and her family; one photo of Thomas Merton; one broadside with images of many Sheed & Ward authors; books by Hasley; a book edited by her husband Louis, The Best of Bill Nye's Humor ; books by Caryll Houselander and Maisie Ward; clippings of articles by Hasley, Sheed & Ward publicity, reviews of her books, and announcements of her lectures.
