Background

Catholic priest; director of the Department of Education of the National Catholic Welfare Conference (NCWC), 1944- 1966; and executive secretary of the National Catholic Education Association, 1944-1966.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Photocopies of Hochwalt's correspondence concerning Sister Formation Conferences (1950-1964), federal aid to education (1944-1964), UNESCO (1944-1965), and the proper role of Catholic educators in the National Education Association (1947-1965); a bibliography of articles written by Hochwalt; copies of articles and speeches written by Hochwalt including a transcript of a debate between Hochwalt and Oswald CJ Hoffmann on federal aid to education, 1961; press releases of the NCWC, 1940s-1950s; a transcript of a CBS television report, "Catholics and the Schools," 5 February 1964; and copies of articles on Catholic educational policy, 1940s- 1950s.
