Scope and Content

Records of the International Confederation of Christian Family Movements (ICCFM), 1955-2016. Pat and Patty Crowley's files consist of minutes and material from meetings, dating from the 1960s and 1970s. Roland and Isabelle Leroux's files, 1970-1994, consist of correspondence to and from and other CFM couples; letters, reports, and other documents involving Leroux and continental CFM couples and chaplains; documentation of General Assemblies of ICCFM (every 3 years) and World Assemblies of Families (conducted immediately before or after General Assemblies); minutes of General Assemblies of ICCFM; minutes of Executive Committee meetings of the ICCFM, 1970-1984; and other correspondence, printed material, and assembly material (agenda, minutes, reports, programs). Wayne and Susan Hamilton's files, 1985-1995, consist of general correspondence, 1985-1989; talks and papers from Avila World Assembly, 1989; periodicals and newsletters, 1985-1989; publications on Latin America, 1986-1989; continental correspondence, 1989-1992; publicity and news articles, 1989-1992; United Nations correspondence, 1989-1995; Vatican correspondence and publications, 1989-1995; Merida Assembly papers and proceedings, 1992; Malta Assembly papers, 1995; first European Continental Assembly, May 1994; correspondence by continent, 1992-1995; International Year of the Family papers, 1994; Asian publications and programs, 1992-1995; Latin America: publications and programs, 1992-1995; Europen publications and programs, 1992-1995; World Council of Churches correspondence, 1989-1995; ICCFM Financial Information, 1989-1995; LINK / LAZO, 1989-2016, with drafts of ICCFM History; Secretariate meetings, papers, minutes, 1989-1995; mailing lists, 1989-1995; and 571 letters on computer disk.
