Scope and Content

Correspondence, 1913-1938; correspondence, financial records, clippings, and dedication program for the John Banister Tabb Memorial, 1925-1941; grade books, 1913-1945; University of Notre Dame School of Journalism bulletins, curriculum, and mailing lists, 1920-1930s; with reflections on women in journalism.
