Background

Liturgist; collaborator with Stephen Joseph Peter Van Dijk in the compilation and publication of The ordinal of the papal court from Innocent III to Boniface VIII and related documents and co-author with him of The origins of the modern Roman liturgy : the liturgy of the papal court and the Franciscan Order in the thirteenth century .

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Unfinished manuscript of Origins of Western Christian Worship: A Study of the Earliest Christian Liturgy and related material including notes, drafts, correspondence, lectures, xerox copies, offprints, mimeographed material, and a bibliography.
