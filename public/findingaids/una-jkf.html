Background

John and Kathleen Ferrone were members of the Catholic Charismatic group People of Praise in South Bend, Indiana.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Documentation of the Catholic Charismatic Renewal in the South Bend area, 1972-1980s; consisting of correspondence, mimeographed and ditto material, and printed ephemera on the People of Praise and Charismatics; also some material on cults, and an anthropological study of True House by Fr. Ken McGuire, CSP: "People, Prayer and Promise" (Ph.D Dissertation, The Ohio State University, 1976).
