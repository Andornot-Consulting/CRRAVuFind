Background

John R. Quinn served as Bishop and Archbishop of Oklahoma City, 1971-1977; as Archbishop of San Francisco, 1977-1995; and as president of the National Conference of Catholic Bishops and United States Catholic Conference, 1977-1980.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Digitized papers of John R. Quinn, with files on the Pontifical Commission on Religious, "Essential Elements of the Church's Teaching on Religious Life," the National Conference of Catholic Bishops, the United States Catholic Conference, the California Catholic Conference, "The Challenge of Peace: God's Promise and our Response," the Papal Visit (1987), bishops' meetings in Rome, his Oxford lecture, the Conference of Major Superiors of Men, the Leadership Conference of Women Religious, Consortium Perfectae Caritatis, and PADRES / HERMANAS.
