Background

Professor of philosophy, founder and director of the Jacques Maritain Center at the University of Notre Dame, and translator of Maritain's works.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence with Paul Bube, Joel Connelly, Nancy Johnson and others, including many of his former students; teaching material; manuscripts of translations, both handwritten and typed, including his unpublished translation of Maritain's Approches sans Entraves (Approaches without Shackles); a memoir involving Maritain; and clippings; with material on modern Thomists and on Evolution.

Approaches without Shackles also on microfilm.
