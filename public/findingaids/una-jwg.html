Background

J. William Gorski collects material having to do with the history of the Polish in America, especially in the region of Detroit, Michigan.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Photocopies of articles (1975-1985) by Lawrence Chominski from The Citizen, Hamtramck, Michigan, on the History of Detroit's Polonia; related articles by Allan R. Treppa, James J. Tye, and others; an inventory of the J. William Gorski collection at the State Library and Archives in Lansing, Michigan; articles by Richard Kubinski compiled by J. William Gorski from the English Section of the Polish Daily News, Detroit; and a compact disk containing The Polish Eaglet, 1981-2010 (Journal of the Polish Genealogical Society of Michigan).
