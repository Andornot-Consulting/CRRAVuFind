Background

Fr John W. Michalowski, S.J., served as a coordinator for the Chicago Day of Renewal, 1971-1973, and participated in other charismatic meetings.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Charismatic Renewal files representing meetings of the Chicago Day of Renewal (CDOR), Immaculate Conception Prayer Group of Benet Lake, Boston College and Cheverus High School Jesuit Community (Portland, ME). 15 audio cassette tapes, 14 from CDOR, 1 from Ann Arbor Eastern General conference, 1977: generally talks by speakers at the Chicago Days of Renewal, including presentations by Fr. James Connelly, C.S.C., Fr. Edward O'Connor, C.S.C., Fr. James Burke, O.P., Fr. George Montague, S.M., Fr. James Keenan, S.S.S., Ruth Carter Stapleton, Kerry Koller and the John W. Michalowski, S.J.
