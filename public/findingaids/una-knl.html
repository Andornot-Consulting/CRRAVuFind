Background

Fr. William (Bill) Kenneally served as pastor of St. Gertrude Parish, Chicago, 1984-2006.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Homilies dating 2001-2006, compiled by Richard (Dick) Westley as Homilies of Faith: Sundays with Fr. Bill Kenneally in the Spoken and Written Word ; homilies dating from March to June of 2006, to supplement the ones in Homilies of Faith ; an issue of Dick Westley's newsletter, "In the Meantime," No. 13, having to do with Fr. Kenneally; and a CD-ROM containing Homilies of Faith in digital files, with audio files in MP3 format for 30 of the sermons.
