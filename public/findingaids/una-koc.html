Background

The Notre Dame Post of the Knights of Columbus, Post 1477, was established in 1910 and was the first collegiate Post within the Knights of Columbus. The Knights have been involved with many charitable events including the Bengal Bouts and annual steak sales on football game days. The Post resided in Walsh hall until they moved into the old Post Office in 1969.
