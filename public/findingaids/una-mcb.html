Background

Brother Charles McBride photographed a number of Notre Dame sporting events. He gave the originals to Notre Dame's Sports Information Department. With permission from McBride, the photographs were eventually transferred to the University Archives.

Black and white and color negatives taken by Brother Charles McBride regarding Notre Dame athletics.
