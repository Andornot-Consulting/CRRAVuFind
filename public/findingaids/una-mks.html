Dates: 1965-1992.

Identification: MKS

Background

Professor at the University of Notre Dame, 1978-1992, associated with the Center for Pastoral Liturgy and the theology faculty; coordinator of Notre Dame's graduate program in liturgical studies and director of the master's degree program in theology.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Files on liturgical, theological or sacramental topics related to his teaching and scholarly research, with a preliminary inventory provided by his wife Barbara Searle.
