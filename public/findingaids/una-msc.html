Background

University of Notre Dame Director of Studies, 1907-1919, 1925-1927; president of St. Edward's University, Austin, Texas, 1919-1925; president of St. Thomas College, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1928-1933; chaplain and professor of philosophy, St. Mary's College, Notre Dame, Indiana, 1933- 1962.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence with James A. Burns, CSC, Knute Rockne, and others, 1919-1938; minutes of the board of directors, financial reports and audits of the College of St. Thomas (St. Paul, Minnesota), 1929-1935; circular letters of the superior general, Congregation of Holy Cross, 1918-1937; decrees of general chapters of the Congregation of Holy Cross, 1855-1912; manuscripts of Schumacher's dissertation, "The Knowableness of God: Its Relation to the Theory of Knowledge in St. Thomas," (1904); and photographs.
