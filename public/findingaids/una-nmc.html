Background

Editor of Catholic Education in America: a Documentary History (1964) and The Catholic University: a Modern Appraisal (1970), Neil McCluskey belonged to Saint Mary's College Board of Trustees during the time when they considered merging with Notre Dame.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Papers having to do with the proposed merger of Notre Dame and Saint Mary's College (in Notre Dame, Indiana) from Saint Mary's point of view, 1967-1972: correspondence, reports, clippings.
