Background

Kathi Novack (married name Skytta) graduated from Notre Dame's Master of Arts in Teaching Program, which she attended in 1965 and 1966.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Memories of her teaching career, her experiences in the Master of Arts in Teaching program at Notre Dame (1965-1966), and her efforts to get permission for women to swim in the lake on campus, as men could do, a permission ultimately granted only because she appealed in person to the president, Theodore M. Hesburgh, CSC; a note donating her photo album to Notre Dame archivist, Thomas E. Blantz, CSC; and a small photo album containing 4x4" color snapshots of the Notre Dame campus and of people involved in the Master of Arts in Teaching program, summer 1966.
