Background

NoVa was a Northern Virginia Christian community in the Diocese of Richmond. NoVa hoped that through liturgical experimentation and active participation of a largely lay community a renewal might be effected and lead to Christian social action.

Scope and Content

Records 1967-1978, arranged chronologically and consisting chiefly of liturgies, with some correspondence and reports.
