Scope and Content

Papers of Thomas F. O'Meara, O.P., consisting of correspondence, including Dominican correspondence and a letter (19 Feb. 1967) from Martin Heidegger concerning translation of a word in one of his essays, with an English translation of the letter and a cover letter by Father O'Meara; a copy of his dissertation; a bibliography and a complete file of his publications up to 2002, including his studies of St. Thomas Aquinas; some ephemeral material associated with his speeches; a set of phonograph records by O'Meara; and a halftone picture of Martin Heidegger.
