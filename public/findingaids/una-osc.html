Background

The Poor Clares were founded in 1212 by St Clare of Assisi. In 1991 Bishop Kenneth Untener invited these Poor Clares to come to Saginaw from their home monastery in Minnesota.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Chronicles, 1990-2018, with the original handwritten version, and scanned and printed versions; files documenting the foundation process, 1989-2003; papers of sisters Bernardone Reining, Laurene Burns, Dianne Doughty, and Laura Hammel; newspaper articles, 1997-2014; correspondence 1976-2018; videos, audio recordings, digital data, and photographs.
