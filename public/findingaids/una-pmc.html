MPMC Paulist Fathers: Microfilm

Early accounts of the missionary work of the Society of Missionary Priests of St. Paul the Apostle (CSP), known as the Paulists, officially founded in 1858.

MPMC 1 1851-1900

Accounts of missions to established Catholic churches in the United States 1851-58, index at 027; accounts 1858-60, index at 029 and 041; 1860-1877 index at 070; 1877-1882 and index at 083; 1882-89 index at 131; 1892-93 at 164; later indexing only occasional, at 202.

MPMC 2 1900-1912

Index at 009. This reel contains only very brief summaries of the results of the missions and their dates, excluding the details of incidents and services contained in the earlier accounts.
