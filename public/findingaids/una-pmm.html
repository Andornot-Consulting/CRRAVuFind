Background

Collected by Sister Patricia Marie Mulpeters, PBVM, during her tenure as the Superior General of the Sisters of the Presentation, San Francisco (1970-1979). Sister Patricia Marie's involvement with LCWR included serving as the LCWR Chairman of Personnel Development Commission, Treasurer of Region XIV of the LCWR, and Chairman of Region XIV.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Agenda, minutes, reports, conference material, correspondence, notes, newsletters, newspaper articles, and ephemera; documenting the formation of the Leadership Conference of Women Religious (LCWR) and its development from its precursor days as the Conference of Major Religious Superiors and Women's Institutes (CMSW); also records of the establishment and early activity of LCWR's Western Region, Region XIV, and records of LCWR's interaction with CMSM (Conference of Major Superiors of Men).
