Background

Mother of Perpetual Help Monastery is one of 40 communities in the international Order of the Most Holy Redeemer. Redemptoristine Nuns live the contemplative monastic life according to the norms of Papal Enclosure. Prayer is their apostolic work on behalf of the Church and the world.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Records of the Redemptoristine Nuns of Our Mother of Perpetual Help Monastery; consisting of member files, prioress files, correspondence files, ministry files, rule revision files, files on Redemptorist houses, writings, reports, financial records, photographs, and realia; with records of changes in contemplative monastic life after the Second Vatican Council.
