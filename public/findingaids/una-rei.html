Background

Adrian and Marie Reimers, a married couple, belonged to a charismatic convenant community called "People of Praise" in South Bend, Indiana. After leaving the community, they spoke out against what they considered to be its objectionable qualities, and founded an organization called "Free Again in Christ" to help former members of such groups.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Documents produced for members of People of Praise, including constitutions and educational material, collected by Adrian and Marie Reimers and by William Beatty; research files concerning People of Praise and other charismatic groups or covenant communities including Sword of the Spirit and Word of God; publications of Free Again in Christ and other cult awareness or cult recovery groups; and related periodicals and audio tapes.
