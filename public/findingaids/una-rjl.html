Background

Priest of the Congregation of Holy Cross; Professor of Theology at the University of Notre Dame, 1947-1953 and 1957-1963; Assistant to the President for Academic Affairs and Director of Financial Aid, 1953-1957.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Transcripts and outlines of talks given during novenas to the Virgin Mary; outlines, plans, and notes on conducting retreats for prep school students, including one given in 1952 at Saint Mary's Academy of South Bend, Indiana; outlines, notes, and articles for a textbook on dogmatic theology; and lecture notes for a theology course in Christian morals, primarily notes on the textbook The Christian Virtues by Charles Sheedy, C.S.C. Also reference files, articles, clippings, notes, outlines, essays, transcripts, etc., written or collected by Lochner for use in classes, sermons, retreats, conferences, etc.; class notes in apologetics, compiled both as a student and a teacher; and various books, pamphlets, and near-print items.
