Background

A native of Kentucky, Francis Clark worked as university microfilmer at Notre Dame; when he died, he left his many collections to the University Archives.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Printed material concerning the Benedictines, Oblate Sisters of the Blessed Sacrament, Carmelites, Sisters of St. Casimir, Sisters of Charity, Cistercian Nuns, Sisters of St. Clare, Sisters of the Divine Savior, Dominicans, Felician Sisters, Franciscans, Good Shepherd Sisters, Holy Cross Sisters, Sisters of the Holy Family of Nazareth, Missionary Sisters, Servants of the Holy Spirit, Sisters of Loretto at the Foot of the Cross, Sisters of the Humility of Mary, Sisters, Servants of the Immaculate Heart of Mary, Sisters of St. Joseph, Sisters of St. Joseph of Carondelet, Little Sisters of the Poor, Marianites of the Holy Cross, Marist Missionary Sisters, Daughters of St. Mary of Providence, Maryknoll Sisters of St. Dominic, Medical Missionary Sisters, Sisters of Notre Dame, Sisters of Notre Dame de Namur, Oblate Sisters of Providence, Our Lady of Victory Missionary Sisters, Daughters of St. Paul, Poor Handmaids of Jesus Christ, Sisters Adorers of the Precious Blood, Sisters of the Presentation of the Blessed Virgin Mary, Sisters of Providence of St. Mary of the Woods, Sisters of Divine Providence, Sisters of Our Lady of Charity of Refuge, Religious of the Holy Union of the Sacred Hearts, Sisters of the Sorrowful Mother, Ursulines, Visitation Nuns, and Vincentian Sisters of Charity.
