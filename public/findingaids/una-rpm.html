Background

Professor of Theology at the University of Notre Dame; author of many books and hundreds of articles and columns on the Catholic Church.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Papers of Richard P. McBrien concerning the Catholic Church, especially changes after the Second Vatican Council; consisting of files on his syndicated columns (1966-2012), correspondence (1972-2011), lectures (1965-2011), television and radio interviews and appearances (1971-2005), audio and video recording sessions, articles (1963-2006), articles by others, book contributions, books, encyclopedia articles, book reviews, courses, interviews; also alphabetical files of topics to support his research and composition of books, lectures, articles, and columns.
