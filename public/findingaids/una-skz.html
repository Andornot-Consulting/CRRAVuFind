Background

Author of several books on Polish Catholics and their parishes, Eduard Adam Skendzel built a large research collection to support study of these topics. Material that he collected on Grand Rapids, Michigan, may be found in the Eduard Adam Skendzel Polonian Historical Collection at the Grand Rapids Public Library.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Chiefly collected material concerning Polish Catholics in the United States, including printed material, notes, photocopies of newspaper articles, unpublished papers, copies of documents from other archives, color slides, and audio recordings; with parish histories and accounts of Polish history in various regions of the United States.
