Background

Parish priest, Plainville, Kansas, 1895-1904, and New Almelo, Kansas, 1904-1950. Vornholt served as domestic prelate consultor, dean and director of Priests Eucharistic League, Diocese of Concordia, Kansas, 1920s- 1940s.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence, 1891-1950, and a manuscript record of American Catholic missions arranged by state and city, 1600s-1900s.
