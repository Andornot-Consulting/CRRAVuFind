Scope and Content

Cold Weather Transportation Technology Project files, including reports, correspondence, task leaders files, awards files, contract documents, budgets, films, reference material, computer disks, publications, photographs, slides, overhead foils and transparencies, mailing lists, presentation brochures, articles, publications, videos of experiments and trials, and a sample of equipment.

Files regarding the Electric Race Car Program in the Department of Electrical Engineering, ca.1994-2003, including folders on publicity, presentations, individual (non-race) events, races, reports, student papers, data and analyses; as well as decals, a few tee-shirts, a banner, and photographs; also race car program files on individual races, technical data, background material, financial records, "Top Hat" and "Nose Cone" from the Notre Dame Formula Lightning Electric Race Car; three banners from the 1994, 1995, 1996 Cleveland races; paddock locator sign from a Cleveland race; signed cap and champagne bottle from the 1994 Cleveland winner's circle festivities; and a signed and mounted picture of Joe Theismann with the car.
