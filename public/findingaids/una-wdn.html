Background

Professor of History and member of the University Council, University of Notre Dame; author of How Firm a Foundation? (1939), Reclamation of Independence (1947), Parents are Teachers (1949), Schools and the Means of Education (1959), The Catechetical Crisis (1966), and The Free City (1967).

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Drafts of books, articles, lectures, speeches (1920-1975); notes for lectures and projects (1918-1975); correspondence (1938-1975); and printed material (1932-1960).
