Background

Author of The Christians of Pakistan: the passion of Bishop John Joseph (London: RoutledgeCurzon, 2003) and Without forgetting the Imam: Lebanese Shi?ism in American community Detroit : Wayne State University Press, 1997.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Papers of Linda Walbridge, a Catholic anthropologist, reflecting her research interests in Christian communities in Muslim countries, especially Pakistan, and in Muslim communities in the United States; with material on the life and death of Bishop John Joseph, the first Punjabi Catholic bishop in Pakistan; including computer files of Linda Walbridge, with images from her research in Pakistan, and email messages to her husband John Walbridge; video tapes of Mosque services in the United States; and pamphlets and books, many having to do with Islamic communities in the United States.
