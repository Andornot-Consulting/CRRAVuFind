Background

Marketing consultant to Catholic publishers and religous orders, sales director at Ave Maria Press, 1957-1964, and general manager of Fides/Claretian Press in the late 1970s.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Correspondence, expense reports, project schedules, and advertising samples from work done for The National Catholic Reporter (1967-1978), Link (1972-1974), Successful Marriage (1973-1975), Celebration (1972-1978), Passionist Missions (1973-1982), Holy Cross Association (1964-1966), Divine Word Seminary (1970-1972), Ave Maria Press (ca.1957-1970), Catholic Church Extension Society (1965-1966), the Claretian publications (1962-1983) U.S. Catholic, Today, and Claretian vocation ads.

Also editorial correspondence, advertising samples, and legal records relating to Fides/Claretian Press (1976-1980); printed material. Correspondents include James F. Burns and Robert E. Burns.
