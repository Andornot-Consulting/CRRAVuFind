Background

Father Edward Sorin, CSC, and Brother Joseph Rother, CSC, served the Potawatomies of the Pokagon Band in Western Michigan from the newly established University of Notre Dame in Indiana a few miles south of the Michigan border.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Miniature photostats made from microfilm of the records of the Michigan Superintendency of the Office of Indian Affairs.
