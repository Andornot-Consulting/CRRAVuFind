Background

In 1967 the bishops on the Board of Trustees of the Catholic University of America dismissed Fr. Charles Curran from his faculty position at the Catholic University of America; the Department of Theology and many others at the university responded with a boycott and the bishops relented and granted Fr. Curran tenure.

Scope and Content

Mimeographed pamphlet, poster, press statement; spirit duplicator handouts; and clippings having to do with the boycott in support of Fr. Charles Curran at the Catholic University of America in 1967.
