Background

Family prominent in the early development of Northern Indiana. Coquillard founded and platted the city of South Bend and was the first white man to live in the area.

Return to the Table of Contents

Scope and Content

Newspaper clippings, obituaries, memorial cards, manuscripts, and a few letters involving members of the Coquillard family and Rev. Edward Sorin, CSC.
