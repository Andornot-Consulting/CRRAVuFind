Background

Brother Agatho entered the brothers' novitiate of the Congregation of Holy Cross 6 January 1917, made his final profession of vows 25 June 1922, and accepted a dispensation from his vows 7 September 1942. Rev. James A. Burns, CSC, an authority on Catholic education, served as President of the University of Notre Dame from 1919 to 1922.

Scope and Content

Typewritten transcription of diary entries concerning Rev. James Burns, CSC, during January and February of the last year of his life, 1940.
