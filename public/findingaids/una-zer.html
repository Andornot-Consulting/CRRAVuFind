Background

Herbert Alfred Vaughan (1832-1903) founded St. Joseph's Missionary Society in 1866. He became Bishop of Salford / Manchester in 1872, Archbishop of Westminster in 1892, and Cardinal in 1893.

Scope and Content

Forty-page diary of a visit to missions among blacks in the United States, 1871-1872.
